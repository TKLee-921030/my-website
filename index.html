About

PHOTO

Taekyu Lee, Ph.D.

I am a Ph.D. in Electrical Power and Energy Engineering from Sangmyung University (2025), specializing in AI-based energy management systems and building simulation technology. Currently serving as Senior Researcher in the Innovation Group at Energy X, leading the development of proprietary BEMS (Building Energy Management System) solutions.

My research portfolio includes 21 publications (including 2 SCI Q1 journal papers) and 5 patents, focusing on digital twin-based building energy management, AI-driven demand forecasting, and zero energy building technologies. I was awarded the Grand Prize at the AI Contest for Sustainable Energy Utilization by the Korea Institute of Industrial Technology (KITECH) for proposing a blockchain-based autonomous energy trading model.

Ph.D. Dissertation, Sangmyung University
